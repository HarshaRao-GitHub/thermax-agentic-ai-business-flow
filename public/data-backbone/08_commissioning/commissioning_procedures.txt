THERMAX LIMITED — STANDARD COMMISSIONING PROCEDURES
=====================================================
Document: TCP-2026-R3 | Rev: 3.0 | Date: March 2026
Applicable to: AFBC Boilers (25-100 TPH Range)
Classification: Controlled Document — Engineering Use Only
=====================================================

PHASE 1: PRE-COMMISSIONING CHECKS
-----------------------------------
1.1 Mechanical Completion Verification
    □ All pressure parts hydro-tested and certified (IBR/ASME)
    □ Refractory curing completed (72-hour controlled heat-up)
    □ Expansion joints and sliding supports verified for free movement
    □ Flue gas ducting leak test completed (smoke test)
    □ All rotating equipment alignment verified (laser alignment report)
    □ Lubrication systems charged and oil levels confirmed
    □ Cooling water circuits flushed and chlorinated

1.2 Instrumentation & Control
    □ All field instruments calibrated with certificates
    □ DCS/PLC I/O checked point-by-point (SAT report)
    □ Safety interlocks logic tested and proved
    □ Sequence-of-events (SOE) recorder verified
    □ Remote I/O communication link tested
    □ VFD programming verified for ID fan, FD fan, PA fan
    □ Emergency shutdown (ESD) system tested

1.3 Safety Systems
    □ Fire detection and suppression system tested
    □ Emergency lighting operational
    □ Gas detection system calibrated and tested
    □ Safety valve set pressures verified and sealed
    □ Explosion vent panels installed (if applicable)
    □ Emergency evacuation routes marked and unobstructed

PHASE 2: COLD COMMISSIONING
-----------------------------
2.1 Utility Verification
    □ DM water quality confirmed: Conductivity < 1 µS/cm, Silica < 0.02 ppm
    □ Instrument air quality: Dew point -40°C, oil-free, 6 bar minimum
    □ Electrical power supply verified: voltage, frequency, phase sequence
    □ Emergency diesel generator tested under load

2.2 No-Load Trial Runs
    □ ID fan trial run: 4 hours, vibration < 4.5 mm/s, bearing temp < 80°C
    □ FD fan trial run: 4 hours, vibration < 4.5 mm/s, bearing temp < 80°C
    □ PA fan trial run: 2 hours, vibration < 4.5 mm/s, bearing temp < 80°C
    □ Feedwater pump trial run: 2 hours, vibration < 3.5 mm/s
    □ Coal/fuel handling system tested: conveyor, crusher, feeder
    □ Ash handling system tested: clinker grinder, vacuum system, silo

PHASE 3: HOT COMMISSIONING (BOILER LIGHT-UP)
----------------------------------------------
3.1 Pre-Light-Up Checks
    □ Drum safety valves gagged during initial pressurization
    □ All drain and vent valves in correct position
    □ Chemical dosing system operational (phosphate, oxygen scavenger)
    □ Continuous blowdown system commissioned
    □ Furnace purge completed (5 air changes minimum)

3.2 Alkali Boil-Out (First Time Only)
    □ Tri-sodium phosphate dosing: 2-3 kg per m3 of boiler water
    □ Temperature raised gradually: max 50°C/hour to 150°C
    □ Maintain at operating pressure for 24 hours
    □ Drain completely, inspect internals, refill with DM water

3.3 Steam Blowing (First Time Only)
    □ Target plate method used for acceptance
    □ Minimum 4 blows per circuit until plate is clean
    □ Acceptance: No impression > 0.5mm on polished target plate

3.4 Load Raising
    □ Gradual load increase: 25% → 50% → 75% → 100% over 24-48 hours
    □ Monitor: drum level, steam pressure, SH temperature, flue gas
    □ Tune combustion at each load point (O2, CO, draft)
    □ Set all PID controllers at each operating point

PHASE 4: PERFORMANCE GUARANTEE (PG) TEST
-------------------------------------------
4.1 Test Prerequisites
    □ Boiler operating continuously at rated load for 72+ hours
    □ All instrumentation calibrated within 7 days of test
    □ Fuel analysis (proximate + ultimate) completed at test lab
    □ Test coal/fuel stockpile of minimum 48 hours arranged
    □ Customer and Third-Party Inspector (TPI) notified

4.2 PG Test Parameters
    | Parameter              | Guarantee      | Tolerance |
    |------------------------|---------------|-----------|
    | Boiler Efficiency      | ≥ 82%         | ±1.5%     |
    | Steam Output           | ≥ 100 TPH     | ±2%       |
    | Steam Pressure         | 45 kg/cm2(g)  | ±1 bar    |
    | Steam Temperature      | 400°C         | ±5°C      |
    | NOx Emission           | ≤ 100 mg/Nm3  | ±10%      |
    | SOx Emission           | ≤ 200 mg/Nm3  | ±10%      |
    | PM Emission            | ≤ 30 mg/Nm3   | ±10%      |
    | Stack Temperature      | ≤ 180°C       | ±5°C      |

4.3 Test Duration
    □ Minimum 8-hour sustained test at rated capacity
    □ Readings taken at 15-minute intervals
    □ Fuel consumption by weigh belt or tank calibration
    □ Flue gas analysis by accredited lab (NABL certified)

4.4 Acceptance Criteria
    □ All guaranteed parameters met within tolerance bands
    □ Test witnessed by customer representative
    □ TPI certification obtained
    □ PAC (Provisional Acceptance Certificate) issued
    □ Deficiency list documented if conditional acceptance
