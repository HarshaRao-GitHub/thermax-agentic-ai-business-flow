SECTION 1: SCOPE OF WORK
1.1 The scope includes design, engineering, manufacture, supply, erection, testing, commissioning, and performance guarantee testing of a complete Zero Liquid Discharge (ZLD) water treatment plant.
1.2 The plant shall treat combined effluent from chemical manufacturing operations including organic synthesis, inorganic salt production, and utility blowdowns.
1.3 Scope includes: Primary clarification, Equalization, Biological treatment (MBBR/MBR), Ultrafiltration (UF), Reverse Osmosis (2-stage with reject recirculation), Multiple Effect Evaporator (MEE) for RO reject, Agitated Thin Film Dryer (ATFD) for MEE concentrate, Mixed salt crystallizer.
1.4 Civil works for equipment foundations and building are excluded from this scope and shall be done by owner.
1.5 The successful bidder shall provide 12 months performance guarantee post-commissioning.

SECTION 4: KEY TECHNICAL PARAMETERS
4.1 UF System: Hollow fiber PVDF membranes, minimum 50 m3/hr per train, backwash provision, CEB and CIP systems.
4.2 RO System: Thin-film composite polyamide membranes, anti-scalant dosing, acid dosing for pH adjustment, flux rate 18-22 LMH, recovery 75% minimum per pass.
4.3 MEE System: 4-effect falling film evaporator, steam consumption ratio < 0.28 kg steam/kg evaporation, titanium tubes for corrosive service. MATERIAL OF CONSTRUCTION NOTE: MEE tubes shall be Titanium Grade 2 (ASTM B338). Tube sheets shall be Titanium Grade 2 clad on carbon steel. Vapor body shall be SS 316L with 3mm corrosion allowance. Heat exchanger shells shall be carbon steel with rubber lining.
4.4 ATFD System: Hastelloy C-276 rotor and stator blades for highly corrosive concentrate. Shaft seal: double mechanical seal with barrier fluid. EXOTIC MATERIAL: Hastelloy C-276 is mandatory for all wetted parts of ATFD.
4.5 Crystallizer: Forced circulation type, SS 904L construction for high chloride environment.
4.6 All instruments shall be Foundation Fieldbus or HART protocol compatible.
4.7 PLC/SCADA system with minimum 2000 I/O points, redundant processors, 21 CFR Part 11 compliance for data integrity.
