SECTION 1: SCOPE OF WORK
1.1 Design, manufacture, supply, erection, refractory, insulation, testing, and commissioning of one (1) CFBC boiler rated at 100 TPH steam output.
1.2 The boiler shall be designed to fire petcoke as primary fuel (80%) blended with Indian coal (20%) as secondary fuel.
1.3 Complete scope includes: Boiler pressure parts and structure, Refractory system, Air and flue gas system (FD fan, SA fan, ID fan, APH), Fuel and limestone handling system (from boiler front), Electrostatic Precipitator (ESP), Ash handling system (bottom ash + fly ash), SCADA and DCS control system, Stack (50m RCC chimney).
1.4 Excluded: Fuel storage yard, coal/petcoke crusher house, wagon tippler, main plant building civil works.
1.5 Turnkey erection and commissioning at site including all temporary works.

SECTION 4: KEY TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS
4.1 Combustor Design: Single cyclone, refractory-lined combustor with water-cooled membrane wall panels in the upper section. Minimum two (2) independent fuel feed points.
4.2 Bed Temperature: Controlled between 830-900 deg C using limestone injection for sulfur capture. Design calcium-to-sulfur molar ratio of 2.5:1 for 90% desulfurization.
4.3 Superheater: Two-stage superheater with interstage desuperheater. Tube material SA213 T22 for primary and SA213 T91 for secondary (EXOTIC MOC: SA213 T91 alloy steel for high-temperature creep resistance).
4.4 Economizer: Two-stage with finned tubes. Material SA210 Grade A1. Designed for 0% bypassing.
4.5 Air Preheater: Tubular type, 2-stage, with soot blower provision.
4.6 ESP: 3-field design, collection efficiency >99.5%, outlet emission <30 mg/Nm3. Rapper system: electromagnetic for collecting electrodes, pneumatic for discharge electrodes.
4.7 DCS: Redundant DCS with minimum 4000 I/O points. Historian with 5-year data retention. Remote access capability.
4.8 Start-up system: Oil-fired start-up burner with auto-ignition, bed pre-heater.
