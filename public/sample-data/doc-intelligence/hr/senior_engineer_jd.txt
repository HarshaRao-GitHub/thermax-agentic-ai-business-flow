1. JOB PURPOSE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

To lead the design and engineering of industrial boilers and heat recovery
systems for Thermax's Energy Division, ensuring compliance with Indian Boiler
Regulations (IBR), ASME standards, and customer specifications. The role
involves end-to-end ownership of boiler design packages — from conceptual design
through detailed engineering, procurement support, and commissioning assistance
— delivering safe, efficient, and cost-optimized thermal solutions for domestic
and international projects.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

2.1  BOILER DESIGN & ENGINEERING
     - Lead the detailed design of water-tube and fire-tube boilers, waste heat
       recovery boilers (WHRB), and heat recovery steam generators (HRSG) for
       capacities ranging from 5 TPH to 150 TPH.
     - Perform thermal design calculations including heat transfer analysis,
       steam-water circulation studies, combustion analysis, and flue gas path
       sizing for various fuel types (coal, biomass, oil, gas, and waste fuels).
     - Develop and review General Arrangement (GA) drawings, tube layout
       drawings, pressure part details, and structural steel design in
       coordination with the CAD/3D modeling team.

2.2  CODES & STANDARDS COMPLIANCE
     - Ensure all designs comply with IBR-1950 (Indian Boiler Regulations),
       ASME Section I (Power Boilers), ASME Section VIII Div.1 (Pressure
       Vessels), and other applicable standards (IS 2825, BS EN 12952/12953).
     - Prepare and review IBR submission documents, stress analysis reports, and
       material selection data sheets for regulatory approval.
     - Interface with IBR inspectors and third-party inspection agencies (TPI)
       during design review and manufacturing stages.

2.3  P&ID AND PROCESS ENGINEERING REVIEW
     - Develop and review Piping & Instrumentation Diagrams (P&IDs), process
       flow diagrams (PFDs), and equipment datasheets for boiler island systems
       including deaerator, blowdown, dosing, and steam distribution.
     - Participate in P&ID review meetings with cross-functional teams
       (instrumentation, piping, electrical, and controls).

2.4  DESIGN CALCULATIONS & ANALYSIS
     - Perform pressure part thickness calculations as per IBR/ASME codes for
       drums, headers, tubes, nozzles, and tube plates.
     - Conduct Finite Element Analysis (FEA) for critical components such as
       tube-to-header joints, manhole openings, and saddle supports.
     - Use Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) tools for furnace modeling, flow
       distribution analysis, and combustion optimization.

2.5  VENDOR COORDINATION & PROCUREMENT SUPPORT
     - Prepare technical specifications, datasheets, and bid evaluation criteria
       for bought-out items (valves, safety fittings, refractory, insulation,
       burners, soot blowers, and fans).
     - Conduct technical bid evaluation (TBE) for vendor offers and provide
       recommendations for procurement decisions.
     - Review and approve vendor drawings and documents for compliance with
       project specifications.

2.6  CAD / 3D MODELING & DOCUMENTATION
     - Guide the CAD team in developing 2D drawings (AutoCAD) and 3D models
       (SolidWorks / PTC Creo / AVEVA E3D) for boiler assemblies.
     - Ensure drawing packages are complete, accurate, and released per the
       document control procedures and project schedules.
     - Maintain and update standard design templates, calculation sheets, and
       the engineering knowledge database.

2.7  HAZOP & SAFETY PARTICIPATION
     - Participate in HAZOP (Hazard and Operability Study), HAZID, and design
       safety reviews for boiler systems and auxiliary equipment.
     - Contribute to risk assessment, safety instrumented systems (SIS) review,
       and functional safety analysis as applicable.
     - Ensure designs incorporate fail-safe mechanisms, emergency shutdown
       provisions, and code-mandated safety devices.

2.8  COMMISSIONING & SITE SUPPORT
     - Provide engineering support during erection, hydro-testing, steam
       blowing, and commissioning of boiler systems at customer sites.
     - Attend site visits for pre-commissioning inspections, performance
       guarantee (PG) test witnessing, and troubleshooting support.
     - Document lessons learned from site feedback and incorporate design
       improvements into future projects.

2.9  PROJECT COORDINATION & REPORTING
     - Coordinate with project management, manufacturing, and quality teams to
       ensure design deliverables are aligned with project milestones.
     - Prepare and present engineering review presentations, design basis
       documents, and progress reports to management and clients.
     - Mentor junior engineers and contribute to building the team's technical
       competence.

2.10 CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT & INNOVATION
     - Identify opportunities for design optimization, cost reduction, value
       engineering, and standardization of boiler components.
     - Stay updated with industry trends, new technologies (oxy-fuel combustion,
       carbon capture readiness, hydrogen co-firing), and evolving standards.
     - Contribute to R&D initiatives and patent filings as applicable.

3. TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

3.1  EDUCATIONAL QUALIFICATIONS
     - Essential  : B.E. / B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering from a recognized
                     university (AICTE/UGC approved).
     - Preferred  : M.E. / M.Tech in Thermal Engineering, Heat Power, or

3.2  EXPERIENCE
     - Total Experience  : 5 to 8 years in boiler design & engineering.
     - Relevant Experience: Minimum 4 years in detailed design of industrial /
       utility boilers, WHRB, or HRSG systems.
     - Prior experience with an OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) such as
       Thermax, BHEL, L&T, Babcock & Wilcox, or similar organizations is
       strongly preferred.

3.3  TECHNICAL SKILLS (Must Have)
     - Pressure part design and thickness calculations (IBR / ASME Sec I & VIII)
     - Heat transfer calculations (radiation, convection, LMTD, NTU methods)
     - Steam-water circulation analysis and boiler performance estimation

     - P&ID development and review
     - Proficiency in AutoCAD (2D drafting)
     - Working knowledge of MS Office, especially Excel for engineering
       calculations

3.4  TECHNICAL SKILLS (Good to Have)
     - CFD analysis (ANSYS Fluent / CFX / OpenFOAM)
     - FEA analysis (ANSYS Mechanical / Abaqus)
     - 3D modeling (SolidWorks / PTC Creo / AVEVA E3D)
     - Boiler design software (HTRI, Aspen Plus, proprietary thermal design
       tools)

       steel design for boiler support structures
     - Familiarity with PLC/DCS-based boiler control systems

3.5  DOMAIN KNOWLEDGE
     - In-depth understanding of boiler types: AFBC, CFBC, stoker-fired,
       co-generation, waste heat recovery, and package boilers
     - Knowledge of water treatment, steam purity, and boiler chemistry
     - Understanding of emission norms (CPCB / MoEFCC / World Bank IFC)
     - Familiarity with EPC project execution methodology
