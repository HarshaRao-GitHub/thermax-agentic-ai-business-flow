1. CODE OF CONDUCT
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1.1 Ethical Standards
All employees shall conduct themselves with integrity, honesty, and fairness in all business dealings. Thermax follows a zero-tolerance policy towards bribery, corruption, and unethical practices.

1.2 Conflict of Interest
Employees must disclose any personal, financial, or family relationships that may create a conflict of interest with Thermax business. This includes:
- Ownership or financial interest in vendors/suppliers/competitors
- Employment of family members at customer/vendor organizations
- Acceptance of gifts exceeding INR 2,000 in value from business associates

1.3 Confidentiality
All business information, trade secrets, customer data, pricing strategies, and proprietary technology are strictly confidential. Unauthorized disclosure may result in termination and legal action under the Indian Contract Act and IT Act.

2. TRAVEL POLICY
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

2.1 Domestic Travel Entitlements
- Grade E2-E3: AC 2-Tier / Economy Air (if >8 hours by train)
- Grade E4-E5: AC 1st Class / Economy Air
- Grade E6+: Business Class Air (>4 hours flight)
- Hotel limits: E2-E3: INR 3,500/night | E4-E5: INR 5,500/night | E6+: INR 8,000/night

2.2 International Travel
- All international travel requires VP-level approval minimum
- Business Class for flights >6 hours
- Per diem: As per country-specific schedule (Annexure-T)
- Travel insurance mandatory — arranged by HR

2.3 Site Travel Allowance
- Engineers posted at customer sites receive Site Allowance as per grade
- Hardship allowance for remote/hazardous locations: 15-30% of basic pay

3. IT USAGE POLICY
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

3.1 Acceptable Use
Company IT resources including laptops, email, internet, and software licenses are for business purposes. Limited personal use is permitted but must not interfere with work or violate any policy.

3.2 Data Security
- All company data must be stored on approved cloud platforms (SharePoint/OneDrive)
- USB drives require IT Head approval for data transfer
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) mandatory for all systems
- Password policy: Minimum 12 characters, changed every 90 days

3.3 Software Installation
Only IT-approved software may be installed on company devices. Unauthorized software installation is a violation subject to disciplinary action.

4. PREVENTION OF SEXUAL HARASSMENT (POSH)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

4.1 Policy Statement
Thermax is committed to providing a safe and harassment-free workplace. All forms of sexual harassment as defined under the Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013 are strictly prohibited.

4.2 Internal Complaints Committee (ICC)
- Presiding Officer: Ms. Anita Sharma, VP HR
- External Member: Adv. Priya Kulkarni
- Employee Representatives: 3 members (rotated annually)
- Complaints: posh@thermax.com or in person to any ICC member

4.3 Complaint Process
- Written complaint within 3 months of incident (extendable to 6 months)
- Inquiry to be completed within 90 days
- Confidentiality maintained throughout the process

5. EXIT PROCESS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

5.1 Notice Period
- Grade E2-E3: 30 days or 1 month salary in lieu
- Grade E4-E5: 60 days or 2 months salary in lieu
- Grade E6+: 90 days or 3 months salary in lieu

5.2 Exit Formalities
- Knowledge transfer completion certificate from reporting manager
- Asset return: Laptop, ID card, access cards, company phone
- No-dues clearance from: IT, Admin, Finance, Library, EHS
- Full and final settlement within 45 days of last working day
- Experience letter issued within 7 working days of F&F completion
