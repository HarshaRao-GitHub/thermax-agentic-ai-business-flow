================================================================================
PROJECT CONTEXT
================================================================================

This BOM is for the RIL Remineralization project (Job No. 630414), which is 
part of the larger 200 m3/hr DM-RO water treatment chain at Reliance 
Industries Limited (RIL) Hazira Manufacturing Division (HMD).

The remineralization system treats RO permeate by:
  1. Passing through limestone contactors (LSF) for calcium/alkalinity addition
  2. CO2 injection for pH adjustment and limestone dissolution enhancement
  3. Chemical dosing (Na2CO3 for alkalinity boost, NaHCO3 for bicarbonate balance)
  4. Hypo dosing for disinfection

This BOM covers the complete mechanical, electrical, instrumentation, piping,
and structural scope required for the remineralization package.
